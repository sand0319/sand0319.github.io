Who

is ARC? We are the Athletics & Recreation Centre. This is an exciting venture for our Algonquin Community. This facility will provide our students with a place to break through perceptual barriers in a welcoming, safe. inclusivea dn inviting environment for student of all backgrounds, culures and abilities to spark a movment.

What

is this going to cost me? This facitit will be included in your activity fee. All fee-paying students can use and participate in all activities in the building. As for the facility, it will cost Algonquin College $48.8 million.

Where
